Common Plumbing & Heating Problems

Plumbing Failure

Experiencing leaks or low water pressure? This may indicate a plumbing failure due to clogged pipes or faulty fixtures. Our team at Pyramid can quickly diagnose and fix it to restore your comfort. Regular maintenance helps prevent this issue!

Learn More

Heating System Breakdown

Cold rooms in winter? A heating breakdown might be due to a faulty thermostat or ignition issue. Pyramid technicians offer fast repairs to keep your home warm. Schedule maintenance to avoid future problems!

Learn More

Poor Water Flow

Weak water flow can signal clogged pipes or valve issues affecting your comfort. Pyramid Plumbing & Heating can inspect and resolve these issues to improve your indoor water quality and comfort. Regular cleaning and maintenance are key to preventing this problem!
